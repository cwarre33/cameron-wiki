## What it is

Train a model to produce explicit reasoning traces — "show its work." Aligns with chain-of-thought and process reward model (PRM) research. Google likely used this to gather data on verifiable reasoning for Gemini fine-tuning.

## Context

"Show its work" competitions reward not just correct answers but interpretable step-by-step derivations. This overlaps with:
- OpenAI o1 / DeepSeek R1 style reasoning chains
- Process reward models (score each step, not just final answer)
- Constitutional AI and scratchpad training
