# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher, educator, and former OpenAI/Tesla engineer. One of the most influential technical communicators in deep learning.

## Key contributions

- **LLM Wiki pattern** (April 2026) — persistent, compounding knowledge base pattern; see [[wiki/methodology/llm-wiki-pattern.md]]
- **microGPT / nanoGPT** — minimal, pedagogical GPT implementations widely used for learning transformer internals
- **min-char-rnn** — minimal character-level RNN, a classic teaching artifact
- **Neural Networks: Zero to Hero** (YouTube series) — builds neural networks from scratch; one of the best deep learning curricula available
- **Tesla Autopilot** — Director of AI at Tesla during the vision-first autonomous driving era
- **OpenAI co-founder** and early researcher; returned briefly ~2023

## Style

Builds minimal, readable implementations first. Prioritizes intuition and mechanistic understanding over abstraction. The LLM Wiki gist is characteristic: an "idea file" that communicates the pattern abstractly, leaving implementation to the reader + LLM.
